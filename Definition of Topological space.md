# Topological Spaces
---

```ad-Definition
Title: **Topological Spaces**
Let $X$ be a non empty set, i.e. $X \neq \emptyset$ and $T$ be  a collection of subsets of $X$ such that
1. $X \in T$
2. $\emptyset \in T$
3. If $O_{1}, O_{2}, \ldots , O_{n} \in T$  then  $O_{1} \cap O_{2}, \cap \ldots \cap O_{n}$ i.e. the *intersections*
4. If $O_{1}, O_{2}, \ldots , O_{n} \in T$  then  $O_{1} \cup O_{2}, \cup \ldots \cup O_{n}$ i.e. the *unions*

The pair of objects $(X,T)$ is called a **Topological Space**. The set $X$ is called the **underlying set** and the collection $T$ is called the *topology* on set $X$. The members $T$ are called the [[Open Sets]]
```
