# Vector Subtraction
---
Subtraction of 2 vectors say $\underline{v}$ and $\underline{u}$ i.e. $\underline{v} - \underline{u}$ is equivalent to addition of $\underline{v}$ and $-\underline{u}$

$$\underline{v} - \underline{u} = \underline{v} + (-\underline{u})$$
