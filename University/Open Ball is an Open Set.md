---
```ad-Theorem

Title:  **Open Ball is an Open Set**

Consider the [[Topology on Metric Spaces]] $T_d$.  
$\forall x \in X$ and **for any** $\epsilon > 0$, 
$$B(x, \epsilon) \in T_{d}$$
i.e. [[Open and Closed Balls|open ball]] is an [[Open and Closed Sets|open set]]
```
